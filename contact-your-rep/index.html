CONTACT YOUR REPRESENTATIVE

Fellow Veteran — We are asking you to do one simple thing: send an email to your congressional representative asking them to support the VFCAA.

Follow the three steps below. It takes less than 10 minutes.
STEP 1 — FIND YOUR REPRESENTATIVE

🔍 Enter your ZIP code below:

TIP
Write down your representative's name after it appears above. You will need it in Step 3.
STEP 2 — OPEN YOUR EMAIL

Open your email program — Gmail, Yahoo, Outlook, or whatever you normally use — and start a new message.

TO:   Type your representative's email address (shown above in Step 1).

SUBJECT:   Support the Veterans and Families Care Accountability Act — VFCAA

TIP
If you see a contact form on their website instead of an email address, use the form and paste the message from Step 3 into the message box.
STEP 3 — COPY AND SEND THIS MESSAGE

Click the gold button below to copy the message, then paste it into the body of your email.

Dear [Representative's Name],

I am a veteran and a constituent in your congressional district. I am writing to ask for your support of the Veterans and Families Care Accountability Act — the VFCAA.

This bill is currently before Senator Mark Kelly's legislative team. It creates a real, enforceable accountability system for the VA — powered by American AI technology — that protects veterans' claims and appeals from being wrongly denied.

I have attached the full bill and supporting documents for your review. I ask that you read them and stand with Arizona's veterans by supporting the VFCAA.
