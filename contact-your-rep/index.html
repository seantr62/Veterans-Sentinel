HOW TO CONTACT YOUR CONGRESSIONAL REPRESENTATIVE

Step-by-Step Instructions for Members — Print and Keep This Page

Fellow Veteran,

You have served this country. Now we are asking you to serve your fellow veterans one more time — by contacting your congressional representative and asking them to support the Veterans and Families Care Accountability Act (VFCAA).

This does not require any prior experience with email or computers. Follow the steps below, one at a time. If you need help, ask a family member or your Post Commander to sit with you.

⚠ Before You Begin — What You Will Need

  1. A computer, tablet, or smartphone with internet access
  2. Your email account open and ready (Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, or any email)
  3. The documents attached to this packet — saved on your device or in your email
  4. Your ZIP code — to look up your congressional representative below
STEP 1
FIND YOUR CONGRESSIONAL REPRESENTATIVE

Enter your ZIP code below and your representative's information will appear right here on this page — no need to go anywhere else.

🔍 Look Up Your Representative by ZIP Code:

TIP
Write down your representative's name after it appears above. You will need it in Step 4 when you fill out the email.
STEP 2
GO TO YOUR REPRESENTATIVE'S WEBSITE
  1. Click the blue "CONTACT PAGE" button that appears next to your representative's name above.
  2. This will take you to their official website.
  3. Look for a button or link on their website that says one of these:
"Contact"  —  "Contact Me"  —  "Email Me"  —  "Send a Message"
  1. Click that button. It will open a contact form or show you their email address.
TIP
Most representatives have a contact form on their website. Some also list a direct email address. Either one works perfectly fine.
STEP 3
OPEN YOUR EMAIL

If the website gives you an email address, send them an email from your own email account:

  1. Open your email — Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, or whatever you normally use.
  2. Click the button that says COMPOSE, NEW, or NEW MESSAGE.
  3. A blank email window will open.
DO NOT CLOSE THIS WINDOW — Continue to Step 4
STEP 4
FILL IN THE EMAIL

Fill in each part of the email as follows:

TO: Type your representative's email address here
SUBJECT: Support the Veterans and Families Care Accountability Act — VFCAA
BODY: Copy and paste the message below exactly as written

COPY THIS MESSAGE INTO THE BODY OF YOUR EMAIL:

Dear [Representative's Name],

I am a veteran and a constituent in your congressional district. I am writing to ask for your support of the Veterans and Families Care Accountability Act — the VFCAA.

This bill is currently before Senator Mark Kelly's legislative team. It creates a real, enforceable accountability system for the VA — powered by American AI technology — that protects veterans' claims and appeals from being wrongly denied.

I have attached the full bill and supporting documents for your review. I ask that you read them and stand with Arizona's veterans by supporting the VFCAA.

"To care for him who shall have borne the battle and for his widow and his orphan."

— Abraham Lincoln

Respectfully,

[Your Name]
[Your City, AZ]
[Your Phone Number or Email]
Veteran — [Branch of Service] — [Years Served]


TIP
Replace [Representative's Name] with the name you wrote down in Step 1. Replace [Your Name], [Your City], and [Your Branch] with your own information.
STEP 5
ATTACH THE DOCUMENTS

Before you send the email, you need to attach the documents that were sent to you with this packet. Here is how:

  1. Look for a paperclip icon in your email window — it is usually at the bottom of the compose window.
  2. Click the paperclip icon.
  3. A window will open showing the files on your device. Find the VFCAA documents and click on each one to attach it.
  4. Attach all of the following documents:

Documents to Attach:

  • VFCAA Complete Bill
  • April 6 Briefing Packet
  • VA IT Infrastructure Oracle Analysis FY2026
  • Veterans SENTINEL Research Outreach Protocol
  • PACKET — Dave Levy, AWS (example company packet)
  • Copyright Confirmation (ECO 1-15132801581)
TIP
If you cannot find the documents on your device, ask your Post Commander or a family member to help you locate the files that were sent in the email from Valiant Heroes Organization.
STEP 6
SEND THE EMAIL
  1. Read over your email one more time to make sure everything looks correct.
  2. Confirm all documents are attached — you should see the file names listed below the message.
  3. Click the SEND button.
  4. You are done. You have just stood up for every veteran in your district.
TIP
After sending, let your Post Commander know you sent the email. We are tracking responses across all nine Arizona congressional districts.
